# About Jun Ives Review

Jun Ives Review is a fictional one-person magazine about images that feel light on the page: paintings, diagram plates, specimen drawings, exhibition rooms, and small archival surprises. The demo is structured like a real editorial site with a lead story, recurring columns, reviews, source notes, and an archive that can support more than a handful of posts. All criticism, events, and editorial details are invented for theme testing. Public-domain or CC0-compatible source material is cited in every article.

This demo uses the `glasswing` theme and fictional editorial content generated for theme testing.
