# The Sound of the Archive Room

![The Sound of the Archive Room](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Archive_reading_room.jpg)

The archive room has a soundtrack: pencil, page cradle, scanner breath, chair leg, apology. None of it appears in the catalog record, but all of it changes how an image is read.

A review site should sometimes write around the object. The conditions of looking are part of the work, especially when the work survives by being handled carefully.

This essay gives the demo a slower pace after the image-led pieces.
