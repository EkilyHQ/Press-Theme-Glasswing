# Print Room Postcard

![Print Room Postcard](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Old_postcard_collection.jpg)

A postcard is a publication with a destination. That makes it more demanding than a print. It has to be seen, handled, addressed, delayed, and maybe kept.

The collection works because the backs are as good as the fronts: crooked names, forwarding marks, and one message that says only arrived raining.

Short reviews help the archive breathe between longer essays.
