# Small Museum Calendar

![Small Museum Calendar](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Museum_gallery_room.jpg)

The best week on the calendar belongs to the smallest room: a print cabinet open for three afternoons, a textile handling session, and a talk about labels that changed after conservation.

A culture site feels real when it has dates, not only opinions. This column gives the demo a recurring preview format that can sit below the lead without feeling like filler.

Recommendation: book the print cabinet slot first. The room only seats twelve, which is exactly why it may be the most useful event.
