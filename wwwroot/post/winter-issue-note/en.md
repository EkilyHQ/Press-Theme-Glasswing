# Winter Issue Note

![Winter Issue Note](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/Winter_landscape_painting.jpg)

The winter issue is about pale images that refuse to disappear: snow fields, empty rooms, silver insects, labels printed in grey ink.

We held three pieces for spring because they needed color around them. Editing is often a calendar decision pretending to be taste.

This note makes the demo front page feel published, not merely generated. A magazine should have weather.
